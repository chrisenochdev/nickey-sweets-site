Meet the Baker

Hi, I'm Nickcol —
welcome to my kitchen.

Nickey Sweets started as a labor of love: baking for family and friends, then for their friends, and soon for an entire community that kept asking for more. Every order — from a dozen themed cupcakes to a three-tier wedding cake — is baked fresh, decorated by hand, and made to feel personal.

"Blessed to make desserts, all are made with love." That's more than our motto — it's the whole philosophy behind every cake that leaves this kitchen. Have a wild idea or a specific vision? DM us or use the form below — we love bringing custom concepts to life.
